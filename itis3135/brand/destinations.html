Greece

From exploring the ancient world to a romantic and luxury side, Greece is a striking land of beauty with endless places to explore. Greece is known as the birthplace of democracy and philosophy with traditional villages and great site seeing locations. There are numerous places to visit and explore to satisfy every type of traveller.

Some interesting facts about Greece include:

  • Greece is one of the sunniest places on in the world.
  • Greece has about 10,000 miles of pristine blue coastline.
  • Greece is home to 18 UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
  • About 80% of Greece is made up of mountains.

One of Italy's priced possessions: it's mouthwatering meals that last all night. Don't miss out on the land of never ending meals along with crystal blue seas, beaches, mountains, cities and vinyards. Cancun

Hop to your next vacation to this extremely tourist-friendly place! Cancun is the perfect place to travel whether it is for a honeymoon, family-reunion or a friends-getaway. Cancun was originally once a Mayan cultural center, and the was essentially used for Mayan fishing and maritime trades. Later on the Mayan's left and Cancun became empty with all of 100 residents living there. In the 1970's, Cancun was rebuilt as the next tropical tourist destinaiton.

Cancun's Top Highlights include:

  • Visit the Chichen Itza.
  • Visit a Cenote.
  • Go scuba diving or snorkeling.
  • Explore the Rio Secreto Nature Reserve.
  • Enjoy the Cancun Resauraunts!

Costa Rica

Costa Rica is prominently known for its lush rainforests, abundance of wildlife and clear beaches. Here, you can hike active volcanoes, surf the warm turquoise waters, and zipline through the rainforests that are covered with clouds. Costa Rica has its own touch of appeal: a relaxed way of living. Residents of the country often recits the common catchphrase "pura vida" also known as "pure life", this philosophy allows one to truly immerse themselves in a good life when visiting Costa Rica.

Here are a list of best places to visit in Costa Rica:

  • Arenal Volcano.
  • Corcovado National Park.
  • La Paz Waterfall Gardens.
  • Tabacon Hot Springs.

Portugal

Portugal has various different landscapes filled with walled towns and castles. Don't miss out Europe's oldest surviving nations, home to world-class wine, beutiful beaches and mountains. Portugal is a place where old and new combine to presne culture and design everywhere you go. Portugal has everything to offer for curious traveller whether it be stopover or a vacation spot.

Don't miss out on these things in Portugal:

  • Visit romantic manor homes in the mountain village of Sintra.
  • See the crystal blue waters of the Algarve coast.
  • Pick up a snack in the Pasteis de Belem and sample their famous tarts.
  • Enjoy some local Vino Porto from Douro Valley.
