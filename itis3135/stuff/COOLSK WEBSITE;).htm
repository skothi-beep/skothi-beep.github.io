What is hiking?

Hiking is an activity that is done in the outdoors. It primarily includes walking short to long distances on narrow pathways known as trials. This activity offers opportunities for socialization, exercise, nature escape, learning, impoving mental-health well being and relfection. Because it is a recreational acitivy it requires a great deal of concentration as some trails my be very rigourous and sometimes even dangerous.



What are constellations?

Constellations are essentially a group of stars that make various shapes in the night sky. They can be interpreted in different ways based on the person looking at it. In simple terms it is like a game connecting the dots. A fun fact is that there are 88 official constellations. Additionally, during ancient times these constellations were used to track the calender.
